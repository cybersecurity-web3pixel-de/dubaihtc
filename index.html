Dubai Herbal and Treatment Centre is the 360 degree solution to a ‘healthier you’

 

Dubai Herbal & Treatment Centre (DHTC) was founded in 2003 under the auspices of His Highness General Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai.

 

Our understanding of health has evolved. It extends beyond the body to include the spirit and mind. Health is personal. It depends entirely on your particular circumstances, nature and goals.

READ MORE...

We embrace this holistic view on health at DHTC and accordingly offer you individualized solutions to wellbeing. This bespoke approach is at the core of our philosophy: we tailor holistic health journeys for our clients drawing from three complementary areas – Care Plus, Get & Stay Well, Feel Well Retreat and Eat Well.

CARE PLUS – Within DHTC represents a new era in healthcare, offering specialized orthodox Western medicine with a patient-centric approach, research-driven excellence, and the integration of various treatment modalities. It is set to be a beacon of healthcare in Dubai, catering to the diverse needs of its community while ensuring that patients are actively involved in their health decisions and well-informed about their care options.

GET & STAY WELL is our sanctuary for good health. We treat a variety of ailments by offering personalized treatments and therapies for both adults and children. As a fully integrated medical centre, you’ll receive the best of conventional medicine and proven alternative medicine under one roof. Our team of both allopathic (conventional) and CAM (Complementary and Alternative Medicine) practitioners are experts in their field, dedicated to their work and sensitive to the needs of each patient. Our unique pharmacy provides a full range of allopathic, herbal and homeopathic remedies.

FEEL WELL – where a Spa transforms into a Retreat, blending alternative medicine and Body & Mind treatments to improve your overall wellbeing. From facials, massages and colon hydrotherapy to guided meditation and Reiki. We will help you to de-stress, revitalize and center yourself. You can also work on your fitness with Yoga, Pilates and personal training tailored to your needs.

EAT WELL is dedicated to nutritious eating. Tuck into delicious gluten free meals that taste as good as they look. Order a la carte or tailor your very own meal plan with vegetarian, vegan and paleo options. It also provides a space to learn about healthy cooking, conduct private events and seminars. Whether you dine from the comfort of your own home or in our restaurant overlooking Downtown Dubai, you can finally enjoy eating the right way effortlessly.

Let us help you cultivate a wholesome lifestyle that will last by getting and staying well in our medical centre, by eating bespoke and healthy meals and by feeling well in our retreat.
